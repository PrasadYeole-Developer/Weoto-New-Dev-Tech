Mobile field operations

Delivered an offline‑first mobile app so field teams could capture, sync, and resolve tasks faster, even with unreliable connectivity.

About

A pilot with one region validated the workflow, followed by staged rollouts that incorporated feedback into better forms and error handling.
Local storage for drafts, conflict resolution on sync, batched uploads for heavy media, and telemetry to monitor crashes and performance.
The app respected on‑the‑ground constraints, reduced failure points, and made the most common tasks obvious and quick.
Drafts
Local storage for offline work.
Sync
Conflict resolution on upload.
Media
Batched uploads for large files.
Telemetry
Crash and performance monitoring.

Challenge

Field staff were losing time and data because forms failed to submit and images wouldn’t upload over weak networks, leading to rework and delayed reporting.
Read also: B2B Ordering

Solution

Implemented offline data capture with background sync, added role‑based workflows that matched on‑site realities, and included in‑app diagnostics so issues could be resolved without guesswork.

Results

Support tickets decreased because submissions were reliable, task closures sped up thanks to background sync, and data quality improved as validation caught mistakes at the source.

    Build a modern warehouse stack that delivers measurable business value


    Digital transformation in manufacturing is a business mandate for CIOs and CTOs focused on speed, accuracy, and return on investment. Leaders in logistics and production operations are prioritizing cost-effective modernization that delivers immediate outcomes. From automating manual workflows to unifying fragmented WMS and ERP systems, today's manufacturing executives need a scalable, interoperable infrastructure that reduces inefficiencies while supporting long-term growth.
    Developing a digital transformation strategy for manufacturing means building resilient systems that can adapt to site-level complexity without introducing risk. This includes cloud-native infrastructure, modular automation frameworks, and integrated reporting tools that inform capital decisions. With the right digital transformation services in manufacturing, organizations can unlock real-time visibility, accelerate throughput, and shorten planning cycles, without replacing their entire stack. Lumenalta’s tailored roadmap enables faster outcomes and measurable impact across the supply chain.

    Solve high-impact use cases in the warehouse & manufacturing industry

    Speed to value means reducing operational friction, without overextending internal teams. Our digital transformation services in manufacturing help you identify performance blockers, align stakeholders, and co-create scalable solutions in weeks, not quarters.
    IoT-powered asset tracking
    Achieve full asset traceability across production and warehouse locations. Connect sensor data across machines, vehicles, and inventory systems for real-time visibility.
    Automated inventory reconciliation
    Reduce manual cycle counts by integrating WMS, ERP, and barcode scanning systems. Align inventory records with physical stock in near real time.
    Predictive maintenance scheduling
    Use telemetry data and AI models to identify maintenance risks early. Minimize downtime and optimize technician resources through timely interventions.
    Integrated ERP-to-WMS workflows
    Close operational gaps between planning and execution systems. Sync production schedules and warehouse dispatch with zero rekeying or lag.
    Energy and utility optimization
    Analyze historical consumption patterns to reduce waste and forecast usage. Drive sustainability and reduce overheads with data-backed strategies.
    Labor planning automation
    Model labor forecasts with actual demand and task duration inputs. Optimize staffing without over-allocating or under-resourcing shifts.
    Real-time supplier coordination
    Connect procurement systems with warehouse intake schedules. Prevent bottlenecks and missed deliveries through timely updates and alerts.
    End-to-end performance analytics
    Surface KPIs across warehouse and production systems in one view. Inform executive reporting and track ROI against strategic goals.

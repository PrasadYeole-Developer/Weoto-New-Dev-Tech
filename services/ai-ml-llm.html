    Digitizing rail yards with AI and machine learning

    An international logistics service provider and port operator offering diverse services, including warehousing, transport, and value-added operations. They serve multiple verticals, including petrochemicals, consumer goods, and art through their seven main business units.

    • Challenge

      Manual and paper-based processes in their legacy rail yard operations hindered efficiency. The company needed to digitize their processes and generate actionable performance.

      Solution

      We developed an AI-powered rail yard optimization platform that digitizes operations and provides real-time planning recommendations.

      Outcomes

      The solution transformed manual, expert-dependent processes into automated workflows, enabling faster training and more efficient operations while maximizing revenue through strategic railcar planning.
      Daily planning time reduction: 10 hours to 15 minutes
      Operationalized railyard logistics via algorithm creation
      AI-led adaptive recommendations for optimization

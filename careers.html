How we hire

Our ability to hire talented people hinges on our clients’ needs. To ensure that we have a strong pipeline of exceptionally talented technologists for when projects arise, we consistently recruit and screen candidates with the most commonly required skills. Our ‘hot’ roles indicate positions with immediate hire needs, whereas our ‘evergreen’ roles are representative of ongoing needs. Regardless of the designation, our hiring standards remain the same.

Upon completion of your application, our team will thoroughly review your profile. If deemed eligible based on the provided information, you will be directed to either undergo a technical test or to participate in a first interview. The order of these two options may vary, but both must be successfully completed before advancing in the process. Next, a recruitment manager will assess your profile, paving the way for your second interview with a technical expert in your applied area. If accepted, candidates can either be hired directly onto a client project, or be placed in our database as an “Active Relationship” until a new client need arises. Please note that the timeline from application to project placement can span between 2 to 8 months, contingent upon the availability of roles.
